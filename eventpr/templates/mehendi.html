Types of Services Offered By Mehndi Artists

Bridal Mehndi - This service includes applying mehndi on full arms and half legs of the bride. You can choose from various styles and designs like traditional Indian mehndi designs, Rajasthani designs, Arabic mehndi designs, Pakistani mehndi designs, Indo-Arabic and Moroccan mehndi designs. Then you also have the high-tech glitter mehndi, mughlai mehndi, and multi-coloured mehndi designs.

Guest Mehndi - All the female guests also put mehndi during the mehndi function. These include family members, relatives, and bridesmaid. In this, mehndi designs are applied only on hands usually. Also the designs are not very intricate. This is done by the junior bridal mehndi artist and the cost is much lesser than bridal mehndi.
