Types of wedding photography

Traditional Photography - Traditional photographers usually aim for posed portraits with the usual poses. This is the age-old “Look Here, Smile Please” photography where the photographer will ask guests and family members to pose for a picture. Since such photographs don’t need creative skills, it comes for a lesser price.

Traditional Videography - This old style videography records the entire wedding event in a 2-3 hours video. It is like a video documentary of the wedding. It is cheap with no creativity involved, as such.

Cinematic Videography - The difference between wedding videography and wedding cinematography is the same as the one between a home video and a movie. In wedding videography, storytelling technique and creativity are used to capture the event and the basic concept is the same as candid photography. Everything is covered in a 20-40 minute video.

Pre-wedding photo & video shoot - Pre-wedding shoots have become a rage now and every couple who is getting married these days is going for it.
